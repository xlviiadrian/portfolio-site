About

With over eight years of experience, I’ve built a foundation in creative direction, graphic design, and media, supported by a broad range of complementary skills. My background extends into music production, audio engineering, video editing, communication, branding, software development, and screen printing, giving me a versatile approach to visual and creative problem-solving. Design has remained at the core of my work—refining my ability to craft aesthetics, develop strategies, and shape visions for artists, businesses, and collaborators. My multidisciplinary background allows me to bridge art, media, and technology, creating work that is not only visually compelling but also strategically impactful.
